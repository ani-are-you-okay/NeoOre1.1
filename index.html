Frequently Asked Questions

How does the pricing work?

We analyze the metal content in your devices and offer you 40% of the market value based on device tier. Simple, transparent, fair.

What device categories do you accept?

We accept all e-waste categories - smartphones, laptops, tablets, hard drives, TVs, printers, servers, and PCBs. Each categorized by tier.

Is pickup really free?

Free pickup for orders over 50kg. For smaller orders under 50kg, there's a ‚Çπ100 pickup charge, which is calculated into your final payout.

What happens to my data?

We offer certified data destruction services. Your devices are securely wiped and you get a certificate of destruction. Or you can wipe them yourself.

How quickly do I get paid?

After we collect your devices, we verify them within 24-48 hours and process payment via bank transfer.

Can businesses use your service?

Absolutely! We have special services for corporates including bulk collection, EPR compliance, and sustainability reporting.

Are you government certified?

Yes. We're a registered PRO (Producer Responsibility Organization) and follow all EPR regulations and environmental standards.

How is my environmental impact calculated?

Based on device weight. Every kg of e-waste recycled saves ~1.5kg CO2, 50L water, and 8kWh electricity.
